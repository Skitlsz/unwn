About Us

Unwined by JC Company isn't just a business; it's a passion project born from a love of good food, shared moments, and the simple act of unwinding. Our story starts not in a corporate office, but around a sun-drenched kitchen table, laughter mingling with the aroma of sizzling sausages and freshly baked bread.
JC, the mastermind behind Unwind, found solace in the art of charcuterie. The meticulous layering of meats, cheeses, and accompaniments was more than just plating; it was a form of expression, a way to weave stories into edible landscapes. His wife, C, a natural hostess with a talent for creating beautiful spaces, saw the potential in turning this passion into something special.

And so, Unwined was born.

Together, they dreamt of creating experiences, not just meals. They envisioned boxes overflowing with gourmet delights, meticulously curated to tantalize taste buds and spark conversation. Each platter would be a canvas, each bite an invitation to slow down, savor the moment, and reconnect with loved ones over the language of food.
